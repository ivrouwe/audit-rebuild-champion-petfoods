Welcome To Champion Petfoods - The World's Best Dog and Cat Food

Steeped in a rich history of more than 25 years working with local farmers, ranchers and fishermen whose values match our own, Champion Petfoods is born from the same lands that provide the Fresh Regional Ingredients for our award-winning Biologically Appropriate™ dog and cat foods.

Our Foods

  • Orijen

    Our flagship brand and the most awarded pet food on the planet, Biologically Appropriate™ Orijen represents a new class of food, featuring an unmatched freshness, diversity and quantity of meats that nourishes dogs and cats according to their evolutionary needs.

    Explore Orijen

  • Acana

    Brimming with up to 75% meat in richly nourishing WholePrey™ ratios, Acana features a diverse range of free-run poultry, nest-laid eggs, wild-caught fish and ranch-raised meats – delivered to our kitchens fresh each day from local farms and waters.
